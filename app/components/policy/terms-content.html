1. DESCRIPTION OF SERVICES, GENERAL INFORMATION AND SERVICES FEATURES.

Helperbit is a platform that allows NPOs, civil protections, municipalities, hospitals and other public organizations to sign up and collect funds addressed to social and public initiatives. These bodies can receive donations in 2 different ways: 1) through the distribution algorithm linked with a specific event, when they are affected or involved in the relief during a natural disaster; 2) through direct donations when they create specific projects. Allowed methods of payments: 1) bitcoin; 2) Credit, Debit and Prepaid Card supported by MistralPay Ltd. The beneficiaries receive bitcoin that they can spend and withdraw. In order to exchange the cryptocurrency in local currency they have to use an external service. Helperbit is not responsible for any mistake or loss. Registered single Users can be added as possible beneficiaries and receive direct donations if they are affected by a natural disaster (among those currently provided by the platform). Helperbit currently provides two kinds of multisignature wallets: - the first solution is for single Users: it’s a 2of3 multisignature wallet where the User owns 2 keys, while Helperbit has only 1 key. - the second one is for organizations and bodies: it’s a 3of4 multisignature wallet where the administrators of the organization have 3 keys, while Helperbit has only 1 key. Helperbit also provides public information about natural disaster events, taking data from third party services, while other information are internally elaborated, starting from raw data. Helperbit does not take any responsibility if there are mistakes and does not recommend the use of such information for other purposes. Making a financial donation you guarantee that you have the full ownership and control over the donation amount, it is free from all liens or encumbrances, and does not exist on the donation amount any legal dispute or other. So when making the donation, you donate pure and free the amount to the selected beneficiaries (and Helperbit s.r.l. could be included as recipient), which accept this act of liberality.
