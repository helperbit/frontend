help and get helped

Many times you will donate to others, but if you complete the verification process and a natural disaster happens in your area you will be eligible to receive direct donations from all around the world

multisignature wallet

We provide multisignature wallet (2of3) to all our users, and a n-of-m multisignature wallet for organizations. This reduce drastically the risk of theft and misuse of the donations.

multi currency

User can decide to make donation with debit card or directly in bitcoin. Organization can accept bitcoin or local currency.

geographic approach

We offer a geographic approach for natural disasters, so you know exactly who is in need and you can help knowingly.

p2p

Some times we just want to give directly to the end user, cutting any intermediary or organization. With Helperbit a real P2P donation is finally possible.

fairness

Many times media coverage is unbalanced and many problems are hidden, we offer participatory tools to compare humanitarian needs worldwide.

efficiency

Every cent is relevant when lives are involved: we don't charge any additional fee if you use bitcoin, the method of payment to use is your choice.

speed

In Humanitarian action every day is important: we offer the oppotunity to send donations in less than an hour all around the world.

transparency

Thanks to blockchain technology donors can track their own donations and organizations can show how they use it.
