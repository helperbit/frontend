Become an Ambassador!

What is the Ambassador Program?

The Ambassador Program has been designed to reward the virtuous community of Helperbit. Your help is essential to spread our initiative and for this reason we are happy to thank you with unique prizes! Everyone can become an Helperbit Ambassador by promoting the platform through their own invitation link.

What is the invitation link?

The invitation link is a customized link that can be used to help your friends to sign up for the platform and which is also used to connect invitees with invitings. Each registered user can find his own invitation link in the "Ambassador" page. If you are not yet signed up, ask to a registered friend to pass you his link, or search on the internet if someone has published his own. To use the invite, just complete the registration form that appears clicking on the link.

Why should I use an invitation link?

Using an invitation link you will be granted with the exclusive "Friends" badge! Only Users who have helped at least one friend to sign up or newcomers who have signed up using an invitation link have the opportunity to receive this digital award and can aim at completing the entire badge collection.

What are the prizes up for grabs in the Ambassador Program?

It is possible to win many prizes, according to the number of friends registered with the invitation link. It is necessary that your friends complete the ID verification process in order to be included in the calculation. These are a limited edition gadgets, only 21 available for each item!

  • 5 Referred Users receive an Helperbit key ring
  • 15 Referred Users win an Helperbit shirt
  • 30 Referred Users congratulations, you have won an exclusive sweatshirt!
  • 100 Referred Users your contribution has been fundamental, you deserve a hardware wallet! (the first 3 are available at only 50 Referred Users, hurry up and don’t miss this opportunity!)

In addition, for the person that has the highest number of referred Users, an exclusive prize: a drone, an action cam or a 72h Bug-out-bag worth over € 500! The list of participants is always available in the "Top Ambassadors" page and the competition is open until 31th August 2019!
The winner must have at least 20 verified friends.


The Program also includes digital awards. The Ambassador Badge is based on several levels:

  • Bronze Level at least 2 Users signed up with your invitation link
  • Silver Level you receive this prize when you reach 20 referred Users
  • Gold Level this badge is unlocked when you reach 50 referred Users

How can I increase the number of referred Users?

You should be innovative and enterprising! There are several solutions to increase the visibility of your invitation link, below we give you some tips:

  • write an article about Helperbit on blogging platforms like Medium and Steemit;
  • record a short video or draw images where you describe the characteristics of the platform and publish your material on social networks. Multimedia material has a very high impact!
  • talk about us in Telegram chats and forums like Bitcointalk;
  • organizes a physical event, for example a meetup, to talk about bitcoin, blockchain and Helperbit;
  • and…. use imagination; you will surely have brilliant ideas, stupefy us!
