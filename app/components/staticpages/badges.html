To stimulate the use of the platform and its diffusion in a fun way, some challenges are proposed here, in order to guide new users to explore the features offered by Helperbit.

What are the badges?

Badges are digital recognitions given to users who complete some tasks. They are shown on the profile page of each individual.

There are 5 types of badges:

  • Trust Badge: this badge requires that you complete every verification procedure in the platform;
  • Supporter Badge: we reward each user that contributes to expand the network of non-profits in the platform;
  • Donor Badge: only the most virtuous users, able to complete technical and social tasks, receive this award;
  • Fundraiser Badge: go into the field, create Fundraising campaigns and raise money for non-profit organizations. The more donations you collect, the higher your level will go up;
  • Ambassador Badge: invite friends to sign up and become a proud Helperbit Ambassador! Your level will grow according to the number of referred and verified Users;
  • Friends Badge: use the invitation link at least once.

How do I win badges?

Win badges by completing the following activities for each prize category.

  • Trust Badge: fill the form related to your personal profile, complete the ID and residency verification and the OTC procedure;
  • Supporter Badge: suggest a non-profit organization that operates in your country, we will do our best to help it to accept crypto donations. Put also 'like' to your best 5 organizations proposed by others;
  • Donor Badge: create your bitcoin wallet, top it up and donate this money to active charitable projects that you like the most!
    • Bronze: you have donated at least 5€
    • Silver: the total donated amount is greater than 50€
    • Gold: you have donated more than 500€
  • Fundraiser Badge: this award is structured in 3 levels:
    • Bronze: you have collected at least 21€ through Fundraising campaigns
    • Silver: you have collected at least 210€ through Fundraising campaigns
    • Gold: you have collected at least 2100€ through Fundraising campaigns
  • Ambassador Badge: invite at least 5 friends to receive the first prize. When you refer 100 Users, you receive a Ledger Nano S! For more information visit the Ambassador Program page
  • Friends Badge: invite at least one friend or sign up for Helperbit using an invitation link
